First 365 Days with Zeus

The History of my Golden Retriever Zeus, and his first year with me. If you would like to learn more about golden retrievers as a dog breed, please click the yellow and blue link below.

HISTORY OF GOLDEN RETRIEVERS


Zeus' First Night

I had been debating getting a dog for awhile. However deciding to get one during the peak of the Covid-19 pandemic, did not make the process easy. Many of the shelters and rescues had no dogs available. However my roommate ended up finding a breeder, and we decided to each get one. We flew out to Ohio, and met with the breeder who told us that Zeus was the happiest puppy he had ever had. We drove almost 10 hours back to our apartment in Massachusetts. Zeus and I both slept for about the entire next day. I had tried to crate train him, but he was not having it, and quickly jumped into my bed.

DATE 7/31/2020
LOCATION Melrose, MA
Zeus' Happiness 5 out of 10

Zeus and his Sister Cali

By August Zeus had learned several tricks including “paw” and “sit”. He had also become potty trained, for the most part. Zeus was also invited to a birthday party, and his sister came. They had a great time together. His sister even dressed for the occasion.

DATE 8/31/2020
LOCATION Haverhill, MA
Zeus' Happiness 10 out of 10

Zeus Apple Picking

In October Zeus and I went apple picking with a few of our friends. We found an apple orchard up in New Hampshire that allowed dogs. Zeus tried to eat about 20-30 apples that day. By this time Zeus had grown to about 30 lbs, and started to look much more like a teenage dog. Which came with typical teenage antics, and having fun causing trouble.

DATE 10/25/2020
LOCATION Hampton Falls, NH
Zeus' Happiness 7 out of 10

Zeus' Birthday

Zeus’ First Birthday was in May 2021, he had a great time. I made him a hot dog cake. He had grown a lot over the course of the year, both in size, and maturity. His size was about 65 lbs, and he had calm down quiet a bit over the year. I look forward to all the adventures that come our way.

DATE 5/31/2021
LOCATION Lynnfield, MA
Zeus' Happiness 12 out of 10
